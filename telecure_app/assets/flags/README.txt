Place flag or language badge images here.

Expected filenames used by the app:
- en.png  (English)
- hi.png  (Hindi)
- pa.png  (Punjabi)

Recommended:
- 128x128 or 256x256 PNGs with transparent background
- Circle-cropped flags or round language badges for visual consistency

Good sources:
- https://flagpedia.net/download (country flags)
- https://twemoji.twitter.com/ (emoji flags as SVG/PNG)
- Or export your design from Figma/Photoshop

After adding images, run:  flutter pub get
